Case Studies

Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Rodrigo used TikTok and Instagram to build anticipation for her music. Her branding focuses on authenticity, emotion, and relatability, which helped her quickly connect with a young audience.

Lil Nas X

Lil Nas X is known for using humor, memes, and viral content to promote his music. His success with "Old Town Road" shows how social media can launch a global hit.

Doja Cat

Doja Cat blends music with internet culture, using TikTok trends and interactive content to maintain engagement and build a strong personal brand.

Independent Artists

Many independent musicians rely entirely on social media to grow their audience. Platforms like TikTok allow unknown artists to go viral and gain recognition without a record label.

Key Takeaways

  • Consistency in branding is essential
  • Authenticity helps build fan trust
  • Viral content can launch careers
  • Social media is now part of a musician’s job
