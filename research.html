Key Concepts

Personal Branding

Personal branding refers to how musicians present their identity online. This includes visual aesthetics, tone, personality, and consistency across platforms.

Creative Labour

Musicians are not only artists but also content creators. Social media requires constant posting, engagement, and self promotion as part of their work.

Audience Engagement

Platforms like TikTok and Instagram allow artists to build direct relationships with fans through comments, livestreams, and viral content.

Industry Shift

The music industry has shifted from label driven promotion to artist driven branding, where visibility often depends on online presence.

Research Insights

Scholars such as Bayton highlight how identity and image have always played a role in music performance, while Hesmondhalgh and Baker emphasize the realities of creative labor in media industries.

Passman explains how the business side of music continues to evolve, especially with digital platforms, and Thomson and Cook discuss how musicians’ work is valued, including non-traditional labor like social media activity.

Social media has become an essential tool for musicians, blending creativity, marketing, and identity into a single platform.
